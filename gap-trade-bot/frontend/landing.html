Get up and running

Start trading in five steps

New to algorithmic trading? No problem. Follow these steps to go from zero to live automated trades.

1

Configure Your Settings

Head to the Gap-Ups tab and set your preferred minimum gap percentage. This filters which stocks appear in your daily scan — higher percentages mean stronger moves but fewer candidates.

2

Identify Opportunities

Browse the Gap-Ups tab to find stocks matching your criteria. Look for strong volume confirmation and clear upward momentum — these are the highest-probability setups.

3

Set Up Your Entry Strategy

In the Entry Bot tab, configure entry parameters: symbol, volume requirements, and your preferred entry time window. The bot monitors and enters positions automatically when all conditions are met.

4

Configure Your Exit Strategy

In the Exit Bot tab, define profit targets, stop-losses, and trailing stops for open positions. This is the most important step — never enter a trade without a defined exit.

5

Monitor, Analyze, and Improve

Use Trade History, Positions, and Stats tabs to track results. Use Backtest to test variations. Ask the AI Chat for insights on what's working and what to adjust.
